Features

Intuitive

Requesting for a delivery is as simple as a click of a button. A familiar user interface backed by the power of the blockchain.

Hives

Where individuals from local communities come together to form an independent Decentralized Autonomous Organization (DAO) based delivery service business, backed by the Ride protocol.

Cheaper

Competition between Hives and existing services would lead to lower cost of service.

Quality

Competition between Hives and existing services would lead to a better quality of service.

Mobile DApp

A full-fledged, self-contained mobile DApp experience that allows on-the-go interaction with the blockchain.

Cryptocurrency as Payment

The ability to pay in cryptocurrency without needing to worry about price conversions.
